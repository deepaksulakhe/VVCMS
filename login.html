About VVCMS

A first of its kind in the country, the Vidyavahini College Management System (VVCMS) is a flagship project envisioned by the Department of Higher Education, Government of Karnataka. The VVCMS unifies and integrates the functioning and the governance of all colleges and public universities in the state under one umbrella...

VVCMS Features

The VVCMS will Digitize the activities of Higher Education Institutions (HEIs)

Everything from admissions and examinations to awarding degrees will be done within VVCMS

We have also included features like class monitoring, lesson plans, and student attendance capture in VVCMS

Even Faculty management features like performance assessment and promotion are included in VVCMS

Revolutionising the Education System in the State

The Unified University and College Management System (UUCMS) is being developed and implemented in collaboration with the Centre for Smart Governance (CSG), Department of E-Governance, Government of Karnataka. The State Project Monitoring Unit (SPMU) under the Department of Higher Education is ensuring the project deliverables, keeping the stakeholders connected in the development of UUCMS.
